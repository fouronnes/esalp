glacier shrinkage in the Alps

2003 – 2016

The two shapes represent the surface area of glaciers in the Alps in 2016, and the surface area of ice lost since 2003. With the Sentinel-2 satellites it became possible to precisely map the footprints of 4395 glaciers in Austria, France, Italy and Switzerland. Alpine glaciers have shrunk by 15% in less than 20 years, and continue to shrink under current climate conditions.
